What is Harmonics?

Harmonics is a digital music box experience where creativity meets simplicity. Each number represents a unique musical note, allowing you to compose melodies by creating sequences of numbers.

  • Intuitive number-to-note system
  • Real-time audio feedback
  • Multiple instrument sound packs
  • Share your creations with friends
